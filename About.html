About Us

We are three University of Toronto students with a passion for web mapping and urban justice. We created this website to show the spatial impacts of CafeTO throughout the city, especially with regards to access and mobility. We hope our map can be a useful tool when evaluating CafeTO's role in shaping Toronto's urban environment!
